courseName: SPORT-REHABILITATION-BSC-MSCI
COURSE OVERVIEW

Gain hands-on skills and experience in a highly professional environment.


You’ll combine theory, hands-on experience, critical reflection and professional judgement as you learn to excel in the latest techniques in exercise rehabilitation.


Our expertise is illustrated by our collaborations with FIFA, the International Olympic Committee and the World Anti-Doping Agency.


You'll work in specialist facilities such as our Sports Rehabilitation Suite, Health and Human Performance Laboratory, Environmental Chamber, Performance Analysis Suite, and Exercise Physiology Laboratories.
