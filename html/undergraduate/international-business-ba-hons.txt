            <div >


                <h2 class="heading-2 -bold -plum-text">
                    Course overview <span class="testUrl"></span>
                </h2>

                <div class="visible-content">
                    <p><strong>We've partnered with one of Europe&rsquo;s leading business schools, ESSEC in France, to create a unique opportunity within this degree that helps to develop the next generation of international business leaders.</strong></p>
<p>On this degree, we'll challenge you to step outside your comfort zone and help you develop the skills to consistently make good business decisions.</p>
<p>You&rsquo;ll learn about the different aspects of global business, with modules on the world economy, European business and emerging markets. Today&rsquo;s managers must deal with different national business practices, cultural changes and language needs. They also need to appreciate how business functions like finance, marketing, human resource management or logistics work across national borders.</p>
<p>Make connections with professional industry networks, like-minded entrepreneurs, and corporate partners in&nbsp;our <a tabindex="0" href="https://www.hull.ac.uk/faculties/subjects/features/business-school-facilities" target="_blank" rel="noopener">recently enhanced Business Lounge</a>. And you'll have access to the <a tabindex="0" href="https://www.hull.ac.uk/faculties/subjects/features/business-school-facilities">Business School's industry-standard facilities like the Bloomberg and Executive Education suites</a>. Or you could help SMEs with their challenges via our <a tabindex="0" href="https://www.hull.ac.uk/faculties/subjects/features/sme-business-clinic" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Business Clinic</a>.&nbsp;</p>
<p>A major benefit of this course is spending a semester at ESSEC, which is ranked in the world&rsquo;s top 1% of business schools, learning from world-leading academics in some of Europe&rsquo;s top industries. This will give you some of the valuable skills and experiences that employers actively look for.</p>
<p>This degree offers real world experience, with our Authentic Business Learning approach which means from day one, you&rsquo;ll get to test your skills out through live business projects, placements, internships, and more.</p>
<p>You&rsquo;ll start with a common first year to equip you with a broad base of business knowledge, which also allows you to switch degrees in your second year if you decide to change direction.</p>
<p>All Hull University Business School degrees have international accreditation from AACSB, which is held by fewer than 5% of business programmes worldwide. We&rsquo;ll prepare you for the challenge of staying up to date in the unpredictable, dynamic business world. And we&rsquo;ll guide your development into a confident, competitive business professional who can take on diverse roles in any organisation or sector.</p>
<p>It&rsquo;s our way of putting you &ndash; and your career &ndash; at the heart of what we do.</p>
                </div>

                          <h3>Personal and Professional Skills for Contemporary Organisations</h3>   
                          <p>Learn about organisational behaviour and HRM concepts. Areas of focus include individual difference, personal development, people management and leadership.</p>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                          <h3>Business Environments</h3>   
                          <p>Examine the environments - economic, political, social, legal, cultural - that businesses operate in and explore the impact of their work on these environments.</p>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                          <h3>Accounting and Finance</h3>   
                          <p>Delve into topics such as financing decisions, cost behaviour, cost-volume-profit analysis, absorption costing, variance analysis and budgeting.</p>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                          <h3>Operations, Supply Chain and Business Practices</h3>   
                          <p>Explore how businesses develop their operations management and supply chain practice. You'll learn the techniques to understand, analyse and evaluate the complexities in today's organisations.</p>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                          <h3>Marketing</h3>   
                          <p>Discover core marketing theories and concepts such as segmentation, targeting and positioning, consumer behaviour, and marketing mix. Apply your understanding of these different concepts in a real-life business project.</p>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                          <h3>World Economy</h3>   
                          <p>Study the changing world economy, developing insights into issues related to international trade, international finance, regional groupings and climate change.</p>

                          <h3>Engaging with Entrepreneurial Organisations</h3>

                          <p>Develop your critical understanding of the role integrated marketing and communications plays in building and sustaining brand identity and equity.</p>

                      <div>
                          <h3>Research Methods</h3>   
                          <p>Prepare and complete a small action research project to develop skills that will help you across your studies. It's hands-on learning about research methodology, ethical approval, data analysis and formulating research questions.</p>
                      </div>

                                  <h3>Placement year modules</h3>
